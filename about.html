About HomeCare Inspections India

Your trusted partner in professional property inspection

Who We Are

HomeCare Inspections India is the leading professional home inspection service provider in India. We are committed to helping homebuyers, sellers, and property developers make informed decisions through comprehensive, independent property inspections.

Founded with the vision of bringing international inspection standards to India, we have grown to become the most trusted name in property inspection services across major Indian cities.

Our team of certified inspectors combines technical expertise with local knowledge of Indian construction practices, building codes, and common property issues specific to the region.

Our Mission

To provide independent, professional, and comprehensive home inspection services that empower our clients to make informed property decisions with confidence and peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and respected home inspection service, setting the standard for quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction in the property inspection industry.

International Standards & Certification

Following Global Best Practices

Our inspection methodology is based on internationally recognized standards similar to those established by:

  • International Association of Certified Home Inspectors (InterNACHI)
  • American Society of Home Inspectors (ASHI)
  • International Code Council (ICC)

We have adapted these global standards to suit Indian construction practices, building codes, and climate conditions, ensuring our inspections are both internationally compliant and locally relevant.
